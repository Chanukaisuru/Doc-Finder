Diseases

General Practitioner

A General Doctor (GP) offers primary care for all ages, diagnosing and treating various health issues, performing routine check-ups, and managing chronic conditions.

Dentist

A Dentist specializes in oral health, diagnosing and treating dental issues, performing cleanings, fillings, extractions, and other dental procedures to maintain and improve oral hygiene.

More Info

Dermatologist

A Dermatologist specializes in skin health, diagnosing and treating conditions like acne, eczema, and skin cancer. They perform procedures to maintain and improve skin, hair, and nail health.

More Info

Gynecologist

A Gynecologist specializes in women's health, focusing on the reproductive system. They diagnose and treat conditions related to the uterus, ovaries, and breasts, and provide routine care such as exams, screenings, and prenatal care.

More Info

Respiratory physician

A Respiratory Physician specializes in diagnosing and treating lung and respiratory conditions such as asthma, COPD, and pneumonia. They focus on improving respiratory health and managing chronic lung diseases.

More Info

Orthopedic surgeon

An Orthopedic Surgeon specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions related to the bones, joints, and muscles. They perform surgeries to repair injuries, correct deformities, and improve musculoskeletal function.

More Info

Ophthalmologist

An Ophthalmologist specializes in eye health, diagnosing and treating eye conditions such as cataracts, glaucoma, and vision problems. They perform eye exams, surgeries, and prescribe corrective lenses

More Info

Nephrologists

Nephrologists are medical doctors who specialize in the care of kidneys. Your nephrologist can examine you, order blood and urine tests, make a diagnosis and treat your condition with medication or a procedure. Your primary care physician may refer you to a nephrologist.

More Info

Cardiologist

A cardiologist is a physician who's an expert in the care of your heart and blood vessels. They can treat or help you prevent a number of cardiovascular problems. They can also specialize in specific areas, such as abnormal heart rhythms, heart failure or heart problems you've had since birth.
